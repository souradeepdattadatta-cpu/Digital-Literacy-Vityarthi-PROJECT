# Task 5 Report – Cybercrime Awareness

I completed Task 5 by studying a cybercrime case and creating a prevention checklist. In Part A, I explored a phishing attack case study where attackers impersonate trusted organizations to steal sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, and banking details. The case highlighted how users are tricked through fake messages and websites, leading to financial loss and data misuse. This helped me understand how cybercrimes are carried out and who is most vulnerable.

In Part B, I created a prevention checklist with practical steps to stay safe online. The checklist included general safety practices like using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication, as well as specific tips for UPI and financial safety such as not sharing PINs or scanning unknown QR codes. It also included awareness about reporting cybercrime through official platforms like cybercrime.gov.in and helpline number 1930.

This task increased my awareness about online threats and taught me how to protect myself and others from cybercrime in daily digital activities.
