# Prevention Checklist (Cybercrime Safety)

## General Online Safety
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share personal information like passwords, OTP, or PIN with anyone
- Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive data

## UPI & Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraud
- Do not click on suspicious payment links received via SMS or email
- Always check transaction details carefully before confirming payment

## Safe Browsing & Communication
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links (phishing attempts)
- Avoid downloading apps or files from untrusted sources
- Keep your device and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for banking or personal work

## Awareness & Reporting
- Stay updated about common cyber scams and fraud techniques
- Report fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call cybercrime helpline: 1930 in case of financial fraud
- Inform your bank immediately if you notice suspicious activity
