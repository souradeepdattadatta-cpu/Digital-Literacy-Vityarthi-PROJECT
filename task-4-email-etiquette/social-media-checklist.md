## Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

### Do's
1. Keep your profile information accurate, professional, and updated.
2. Use strong privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal details.
3. Verify news, screenshots, and external links before sharing them publicly.
4. Communicate respectfully in comments, messages, and academic discussions.
5. Use social media to highlight learning, projects, achievements, and constructive interests.

### Don'ts
1. Do not share passwords, OTPs, banking details, or other sensitive personal information.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
3. Do not accept connection requests from unknown people without checking authenticity.
4. Do not post real-time travel details, live location, or private routine information publicly.
5. Do not trust suspicious giveaways, job offers, or urgent links without verification.
