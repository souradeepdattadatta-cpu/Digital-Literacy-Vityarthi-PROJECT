# Project Report – Digital Communication Scenario

Poor digital communication can lead to serious misunderstandings. For example, a student once sent an email to a professor requesting an assignment extension but used an unclear subject line, informal language, and did not properly explain the reason. As a result, the professor ignored the email, assuming it was unimportant or unprofessional. This caused the student to miss the deadline and lose marks.

Similarly, on social media, posting inappropriate or unclear content can harm a student’s reputation and create negative impressions among peers and teachers.

This situation could have been handled better by following proper email etiquette, such as using a clear subject line, a polite tone, and a well-structured message. On social media, maintaining professionalism, thinking before posting, and avoiding offensive content are essential. Clear, respectful, and responsible communication helps prevent such problems.
