# Project Report: Digital Literacy Task

I used **HackerRank and Google Forms** as part of this task to enhance my digital literacy and technical skills. **HackerRank** is a widely used coding platform that helps students practice programming through problem-solving. I solved beginner-level problems, which helped me understand basic programming concepts such as input-output operations, functions, and logical thinking. It also improved my problem-solving ability and gave me confidence in writing code efficiently.

In addition, I created a **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using **Google Forms**. The form included a mix of multiple-choice and short-answer questions to test knowledge about online safety, strong passwords, phishing, and email etiquette. I also learned how to share the form, collect responses, and view them in Google Sheets for analysis.

These tools will be highly beneficial for my academic growth. HackerRank will help me regularly practice coding, which is essential for exams, assignments, and technical interviews. Google Forms will assist me in conducting surveys, collecting data, and presenting results in an organized way for projects. Overall, using these platforms will strengthen my technical, analytical, and practical skills over the next few years.
