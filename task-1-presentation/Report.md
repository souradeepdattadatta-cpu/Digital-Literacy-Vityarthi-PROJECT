# Project Report: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Author:** Souradeep Datta\
**Task:** 1 (Module 1)

## Tool Description

For this task, I used Canva to design a one-page digital literacy
infographic. I selected this tool because it offers a wide range of
educational icons, templates, and a flexible grid system. These features
helped me create a clean, visually appealing, and well-structured
layout, which is essential for presenting awareness-based content
effectively.

## Design Overview

The infographic is titled **"Digital Literacy Awareness"** and is
divided into three clearly defined vertical sections to ensure a logical
and easy-to-follow flow of information:

1.  **What is Digital Literacy?**\
    This section introduces the concept of digital literacy, emphasizing
    the ability to find, evaluate, and use digital information
    responsibly and effectively.

2.  **Safe Internet Practices**\
    The central section highlights important online safety measures. It
    uses relevant icons to represent key ideas such as strong password
    creation, phishing awareness, and responsible use of applications
    and websites.

3.  **Email Etiquette**\
    The final section presents professional email practices in a
    checklist format. It includes points such as writing clear subject
    lines, maintaining a formal tone, and using appropriate greetings
    and closings.

The overall design follows a modern blue and white color palette, which
enhances readability and gives the infographic a clean, professional
academic appearance suitable for student audiences.

## Reflection

One aspect I found particularly interesting during this process was
understanding the importance of **visual hierarchy**. As a student
specializing in AI and Machine Learning, I am accustomed to working with
raw data and technical concepts. However, converting those ideas into
simple, visually engaging, and easily understandable content required a
different approach to thinking.

Balancing text, icons, and whitespace to ensure clarity and engagement
was a valuable learning experience. This task improved my ability to
communicate complex ideas in a concise and visually effective manner,
which is an essential skill in both academic and professional
environments.
