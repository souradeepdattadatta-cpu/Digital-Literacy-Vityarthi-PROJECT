# Digital Literacy Portfolio Report

I have chosen GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle as the key platforms to develop my digital presence and professional portfolio. Each of these platforms plays an important role in building my skills and career.

GitHub is a platform for hosting and sharing code. I will use it to upload my projects, practice programming, and collaborate with others. Over the next four years, I plan to create a strong portfolio that reflects my growth in software development and problem-solving.

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where I can connect with industry professionals, showcase my achievements, and stay updated with industry trends. I will use it to build a professional profile and explore internship and job opportunities.

Kaggle is a platform focused on data science and machine learning. I will use it to work with datasets, build models, and participate in competitions to improve my analytical skills.

Over the next four years, I aim to consistently learn, practice, and actively use these platforms to enhance my knowledge, build projects, and prepare for my future career.
